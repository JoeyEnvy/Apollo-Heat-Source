Welcome to Apollo Heat Source

Founded and run by Chris Needham, Apollo Heatsource is a small, family-run business based in Doncaster, South Yorkshire, specializing in wood burning stoves and fireplace installation and also flue system installations and rectifications. With years of hands-on experience, we take pride in delivering high-quality craftsmanship, ensuring every home is fitted with a stove or fireplace that's not only functional but also a stunning focal point.

Whether you're looking to install a brand-new log burner, upgrade your existing fireplace, or create a completely bespoke fireplace surround, we're here to help. We have many options to suit most budgets.

Our services cover everything from supplying and fitting wood and multi-fuel stoves to building custom fireplace designs that add warmth and character to your home.

We also have exclusive use of our supplier partners' beautiful showroom, which is based in Mexborough. You can visit the showroom and browse at your leisure without any of the sales pressure. You will find the staff knowledgeable and very helpful, liaising with ourselves to find you the perfect fireplace for your home.

At a time of rising utility bills, we can provide an environmentally friendly, carbon-neutral source of renewable energy through our wide range of modern eco-design wood burning stoves. We also equip you with the best advice and guidance on how to get the most out of your new appliance, sourcing and storing your wood fuel and yearly servicing, ensuring your appliance runs smoothly for years to come.

At Apollo Heatsource, customer satisfaction comes first. Don't just take our word for it—our loyal customers return time and time again, trusting us to bring warmth, comfort, and style to their homes.

Let Chris and the team at Apollo Heatsource create the perfect fireplace or stove setup for your home. Get in touch today!

How often should I have my chimney swept?

We recommend having your chimney swept at least once a year, or more frequently if you use your fireplace or stove regularly. Here's a general guide:

  • Wood burning: At least once per season of use
  • Coal burning: At least twice per season of use
  • Oil or gas: Once a year
  • Pellet stoves: Once a year
